🐠 The Golden Fish Mystery

🔍 A Detective Story for Young Investigators

Welcome, Detective!

Something terrible happened at Oakwood School yesterday afternoon. The beautiful golden fish, Sunny, who lived in the big aquarium in the Biology classroom, has disappeared! The aquarium is empty. There is water on the floor, and the glass has a small crack.

Sunny was the school's favourite pet. Every student loved to watch her swim. The aquarium was locked, but someone broke it. The crime happened between 2:00 PM and 3:00 PM yesterday. During that time, the school was almost empty, but some people were still here.

Three suspects were at school at that time. Each of them has a story. But... is their story true? Who is lying? Who took Sunny?

Your job is simple:
• Visit the crime scene and two other locations.
• Ask questions and collect information in your Detective Notebook.
• Find the thief and their motive!
